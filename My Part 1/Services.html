We are a customer-focused energy company, fully owned by the City of Johannesburg, operating within a regulated framework. Our mission is to ensure that everyone in the city has access to reliable electricity, while also working to expand and diversify our energy sources. We build, run, and maintain the citys electricity infrastructure with care for the environment and a commitment to sustainability. Serving one of Africas largest metros,we are proud to help improve the safety and quality of life for Johannesburgs residents, powering the citys growth and development every step of the way.
